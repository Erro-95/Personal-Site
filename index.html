SPECIALTIES

Responsive Design

Websites should be designed with a mobile first approach. Not everyone has access to a desktop computer but most people do in fact have access to some sort of mobile device, albeit a tablet, cellphone or even a smart watch.

Simplicity

I believe in simple design. Websites should be as clutter-free as possible, allowing for a much more pleasant experience. When navigating through a site, transitions should be seamless and simple amd should not intrude the user experience.
